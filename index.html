Child Sexual Abuse Material Prevention Learning Platform

Welcome to our CSAM educational awareness website, where knowledge becomes power in the fight against online exploitation.

    Our primary goal is to illuminate the severity of CSAM online and to explore technological and educational strategies for its prevention. Central to our investigation are the following questions:
  • What actions are social media platforms currently taking to address CSAM, and what are the benefits and drawbacks of these strategies?
  • How can we balance child safety online with the imperative of user privacy?
  • What improvements can be made in current strategies, and how can public perception be shifted towards a more proactive stance against CSAM?

AI-generated CSAM presents unique challenges for law enforcement and digital forensics experts due to its ability to evade traditional detection methods and forensic analysis techniques.

Fact #3

In recent years, there has been a surge in the use of AI-generated CSAM by online predators to exploit and manipulate vulnerable individuals, including children and adolescents.
