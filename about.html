Our Mission

Research on Social Media Platforms and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Our study focuses on evaluating social media platforms' current measures to combat CSAM online. We aim to understand their effectiveness, limitations, and potential enhancements. Through our research, we seek to raise awareness, assess public attitudes, and develop strategies to create a safer online environment.

Understanding CSAM

Our primary goal is to illuminate the severity of CSAM online and to explore technological and educational strategies for its prevention.

Analyzing Platform Actions

We investigate actions social media platforms are currently taking to address CSAM, evaluating their benefits and drawbacks.

Enhancing Strategies

We aim to identify improvements in current strategies and shift public perception towards a proactive stance against CSAM.
